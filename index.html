Welcome to the Toybox!

This website is a small passion project of mine. I wanted to review html and css, learn more, and make something fun for myself that others can enjoy. I actually got the inspiration to make this website from a youtube video talking about the indie web and how it brings back the nostalgia of the old internet before everything changed. Growing up around an older brother playing gamecube and ps2 games, I grew fond of what I would call "retro media." I wanted to be part of that creative and nostalgic side of things. Whether it's sucking up a ghost in Luigi's Mansion or wacking someone with a Keyblade in Kingdom hearts -- these are the memories that are going to stick with me for the rest of my life. I want to preserve that love and passion whether it is old games or whether it's the harsh blood reds and mustard yellow websites that ruin your eyes forever.

Rant One

For the average user, a tech inconvenience can be mildly annoying, but for an IT guy -- those difficulties are something we have to put up with every single day. Even something like AI can be incredibly frustrating as it presents dated information, ignores user set parameters, or simply just fails to understand what the user is asking for. It also creates the moral dilemma of balancing helping a student and doing the whole assignment for them. When AI does everything for you, you learn little to nothing.
